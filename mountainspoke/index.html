Cycle the Continental Divide

The Continental Divide National Scenic Trail is a trail system that passes through five states in the U.S. - Montana, Idaho, Wyoming, Colorado, and New Mexico. It runs 3,100 miles between Mexico and Canada, along the Continental Divide of the Americas including the Rocky Mountains, and is known as the United States National Scenic Trail.

Every year we seek the most committed of adventurers to join us along the trail. Are you ready for the journey of a lifetime?
