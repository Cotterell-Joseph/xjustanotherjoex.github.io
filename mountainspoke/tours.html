Daily Adventures

Mini Continental Divide

Dont' have a lot of time? but have always wanted to cycle the Continental Divide? We have you covered. Join us for a guided all-day tour. We'll put in just north of Butte, Montana where the Continental Trail meets Interstate 15. From there we will follow the trail approx 50 miles, where we will be picked up. You are likely to be famished, so we will make a dinner stop in Butte, where you will be able to choose from local favorites, such as Casagranda'a Steakhouse and Sparky's Garage.

Skill Level: Moderate
Cost: $85

Bozeman Beast

So you're fearless. You haven't found a trail too difficult for you? We think the Bozeman Beast is right up your alley. With a combined distance just under 20 miles, there will be plenty of time to explore alternate paths, explore multiple paths, and perform technical feats that would make your agent wet himself. This half-day adventure ends at the Fallen Gravestone rock garden, a perfect end to a perfect tour.

Skill Level: Difficult
Cost: $95
